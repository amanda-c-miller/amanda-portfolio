Archive Vision is a mobile-first application that allows historians and archivists to scan historial documents, extract text, and query them using semantic search similarity. As product manager, I work directly with Professor Seth Bernstein (Ph.D. University of Toronto, 2013) to tailor the application to his technical requirements. I designed the product backlog, scope of work, system architecture and oversee a team of 4 students across backend and frontend using the agile framework. The final product will be delivered to the UF History Department for use digital humanities projects and for ongoing development.

Our tech stack includes Docker, FastAPI, Celery/Redis, MariaDB, and Chromadb. The frontend is built with React Native for compatibility on Android and iOS devices.
